About Siana Construction

Delivering premium construction and engineering solutions across Kenya

Introduction

Siana Construction and Engineering Limited is emerging as a leading provider of construction and engineering services in Kenya with an exemplary record of quality and timely construction solutions. We operate in:

  • General Building Works
  • Construction of small dams and pans
  • Construction of roads and bridges
  • Electrical installations and structured cabling
  • Mechanical installations and services

Licensed by the National Construction Authority in the following categories:

  • Buildings Works - Category NCA 4
  • Road Works - Category NCA 4
  • Water works - Category NCA 4
  • Electrical Engineering Services - Category NCA 4
  • Mechanical Engineering Services - Category NCA 4
  • Access to Government Procurement Opportunities (AGPO)
